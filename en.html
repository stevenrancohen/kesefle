How it works

Three steps. Most users finish setup in under 90 seconds.

1

Sign in with Google

We create a private Google Sheet in your Drive. Only you can see it. We touch only files we made (drive.file scope — Google's most restricted).

2

Connect WhatsApp

One tap on our WhatsApp number. Say "hi". You're in. The bot remembers you for next time.

Text your expenses

Common questions

Is the app actually free?⌄

Yes. Free tier covers up to 30 expenses/month — most personal users never exceed that. Pro (₪19/mo) unlocks unlimited, history, custom categories, Sunday digest, ROAS panel, CSV/PDF export. 14-day Pro trial, no card needed.

Will it work for my business too?⌄

Yes — solo freelancers and small businesses are our biggest segment. Add "P" or "פטור" suffix to mark business expenses; we pre-populate a profitability/cash-flow sheet template. Green Invoice integration on the roadmap.

What about receipts?⌄

Send a photo of a receipt to the bot. We extract amount, vendor, and date, classify the category, and log it. (Available in Pro; OCR powered by Gemini Vision.)

Can my partner / accountant see the sheet?⌄

It's your Google Sheet — share it with anyone you want from inside Drive. Family plan (₪39/mo) lets up to 4 phones write to one shared sheet with per-person color tags.

What if I want to delete everything?⌄

Open Account → Delete account. We revoke OAuth, drop everything from our database, and leave your sheet exactly where it is (you can keep it or delete it from Drive). Full audit log preserved per Israeli Privacy Law Amendment 13.

Why WhatsApp and not Telegram / iMessage?⌄

90%+ of Israelis use WhatsApp daily. Telegram and iMessage are on the roadmap — vote for them at /roadmap.
