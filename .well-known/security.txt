# Responsible disclosure:
# - Please give us 90 days to remediate before public disclosure.
# - For critical issues (CVSS >= 9), we aim to respond within 24 hours.
# - We do not yet run a paid bug bounty, but credit is given on /trust.

# Out of scope:
# - Social engineering of staff or users.
# - Physical attacks on infrastructure.
# - Denial of service against rate-limited endpoints.
# - Reports requiring physical access to a victim's device.

# In scope:
# - Authentication bypass on /api/* endpoints.
# - Information disclosure (especially of refresh tokens, sheets data).
# - HMAC verification bypass on /api/whatsapp/webhook and /api/billing/webhook.
# - Formula injection through sheet writes.
# - Account takeover via OAuth flow.
