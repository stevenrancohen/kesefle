# Kesefle UX Audit — first-time-visitor walkthrough
**Date:** 2026-05-26
**Pages walked:** index, account, dashboard, pricing, welcome, about, trust, help, demo, contact
**Auditor lens:** a non-technical Israeli who landed on kesefle.com from a Facebook ad, has never used the product, and is deciding in 60 seconds whether to trust it with their money.

---

## 1. Executive summary — top 5 highest-leverage fixes

Ranked by **impact ÷ effort**. All are "tiny patch, big lift in trust/conversion".

| # | Fix | Where | Effort | Why it matters |
|---|-----|-------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | **Reconcile the free-trial claim across the site** — homepage promises "חודש ראשון חינם" 5+ times, pricing page promises "14 ימים", JSON-LD FAQ says "בתקופת השקה כולם משתמשים בחינם ב-Pro". Pick one and use it everywhere. | `index.html:116,712,1446,1475,1530` vs `pricing.html:313,388,406,431,860,943` | 15 min find-and-replace | This is the single biggest trust killer on the site. A visitor who clicks "התחל ניסיון של חודש" from the homepage and lands on a "14 ימים" pricing CTA will assume bait-and-switch and bounce. |
| 2 | **Fix the contact-page phone-number split** — `contact.html` lists Israeli `054-776-0643` as the WhatsApp number while every other page (homepage, dashboard, welcome, about, trust, help, pricing, demo) sends users to US `+1-555-640-8123`. A user who saves the contact number then writes to the bot at that number will get nothing back. | `contact.html:192,197` | 5 min | Silent dead-end for support — exactly the moment users need to reach you. Plus it makes us look unprofessional. |
| 3 | **Invisible / illegible CTAs.** Three confirmed contrast bugs: (a) welcome.html toast `background:#ffffff;color:white` → invisible "הועתק" feedback; (b) mobile hamburger "התחל חינם" on about.html:337 + contact.html:175 is `bg-brand-600 text-ink-900` → dark navy text on indigo, almost unreadable; (c) welcome.html:126 "תחגוג איתי" button is `bg-white text-ink-900 dark:bg-white dark:text-ink-900` and sits on the white hero — visually missing. | `welcome.html:126,568`, `about.html:337`, `contact.html:175` | 10 min | Every one of these is a CTA users physically cannot see. Cheap to fix; pure conversion gain. |
| 4 | **Replace the homepage's invented "live stats strip"** — `18,725` words / `389` categories / `99%` accuracy with no source. A skeptical user will Google this and find nothing. Combined with the just-removed testimonials section, the homepage now has zero verifiable trust signals. Replace with **honest** numbers (e.g. "מילון של 1,480 מילים" matches help.html:627) and one or two real artifacts: "הקוד שלי באוויר ב-`/about#timeline`", a SOC-Lite badge linking to `/trust`, or a "100 משתמשים פעילים" stat sourced from `/about#timeline`. | `index.html:836-854` + general | 30 min | The homepage post-testimonial-removal has a credibility gap. Filling it with truthful numbers is faster than waiting for testimonials. |
| 5 | **Decide the brand bot's primary phone display + add it to the homepage.** Today the homepage never tells visitors *what number to write to* — they have to click "התחל", finish OAuth, provision a sheet, and reach `/welcome` before the number `+1-555-640-8123` is finally exposed. A simple "המספר של הבוט: +1-555-640-8123" chip on the homepage (with `wa.me` deep link) is the missing zero-friction trial. | new chip in `index.html` hero | 15 min | The whole pitch is "just text the bot". Letting people try without signup is the ultimate "show, don't tell" — and the existing playground already gives 80% of the wow without it. |

### 3.7 Six dropdown nav across 7 pages = decision fatigue

**Total nav choices visible on hover: ~20.** For a Hebrew SaaS homepage targeting non-technical buyers this is heavy.

**Recommendation:** Compress to 4: "המוצר" (איך עובד, דמו, מחירים) / "למי" (משפחות, עצמאים, קבוצות) / "מדריכים" (עזרה, בלוג, סטטוס) / "אודות". Move "צור קשר" to the footer + a single "התחברות" right CTA.
