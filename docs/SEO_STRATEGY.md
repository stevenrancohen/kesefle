# Kesefle — Organic Growth Strategy (honest version)

You asked me to never make you think about SEO again. The most useful thing I
can do is run the parts that actually work and be straight about the parts that
would **backfire** — because doing SEO wrong now is worse than doing nothing.

## What actually moves the needle (we DO this)
1. **Technical SEO** — fast pages, clean titles/meta, one H1, canonical, mobile,
   sitemap, internal links. (Largely done; ongoing.)
2. **Structured data (schema.org)** — so Google understands the brand, product,
   FAQs, how-to. (Brand/WebSite/SoftwareApplication/FAQ done on homepage; rolling
   out to more pages.)
3. **Brand association** — "כספלה" now in schema `alternateName`; used in copy.
4. **A few genuinely useful pages** — `/freelancers`, `/faq`, real comparison —
   each unique and valuable.
5. **Quality content at a sane cadence** — 1–2 well-researched posts/week,
   reviewed before publish.

## What we deliberately DON'T do (and why)
These are in your brief but would get the site **demoted** under Google's 2024
spam policies — I won't ship harm that looks like progress:

- **2 auto-generated posts/day, auto-published** → "scaled content abuse." Mass
  AI content with no human review is a top demotion trigger since March 2024.
- **10+ near-duplicate city pages** ("מעקב הוצאות תל אביב/חיפה/…") → "doorway
  pages," an explicit spam violation. One honest "works across Israel" page
  instead.
- **Auto-built backlinks / mass directory submissions** → "link spam." Can
  trigger manual actions. Real links come from real mentions.
- **Fake Google Business Profile / local citations** → Kesefle is a nationwide
  SaaS, not a storefront; a fake local listing violates GBP policy and can be
  removed/penalized.
- **Auto-posting to social via API / creating accounts** → I don't create
  accounts on your behalf, and there's no connected social API. I'll generate a
  *content queue* you can post.

## The realistic timeline (so expectations are right)
- Ranking for **"כספלה"** itself: needs the site **indexed** + a little time +
  trust signals. Schema + sitemap help Google connect the name; it won't be #1
  the same day. The creperie/Wikipedia results outrank you today purely because
  they're older/established — that erodes as your pages get indexed and linked.
- Commercial keywords ("מעקב הוצאות וואטסאפ"): weeks-to-months with steady
  quality content + links. There is no instant button.

## 🔴 What only YOU can do (do these — they unlock everything)
1. **Google Search Console**: go to search.google.com/search-console → add
   property `kesefle.com` → verify (DNS TXT or the HTML tag) → **Sitemaps →
   submit `https://kesefle.com/sitemap.xml`**. Without this, Google indexes
   slowly and you're blind to what's happening.
2. **Request indexing** for the homepage + key pages in GSC (URL Inspection →
   Request Indexing).
3. **Analytics**: add GA4 or Plausible (give me the snippet/site-id and I'll
   wire it into every page) so growth is measurable.
4. **One real backlink** to start: your personal LinkedIn/X posting about
   launching Kesefle, a Product Hunt / Geektime mention, a relevant Facebook
   group. One genuine mention beats 50 spam links.
5. **Bing Webmaster Tools** (5 min, same sitemap) — easy extra traffic.

## How the automation works (safe version)
A recurring **SEO audit agent** runs and writes a report (titles/meta/schema/
broken links/sitemap coverage + Lighthouse-style checks) and **proposes** fixes.
It does **not** auto-publish content to production — content goes through review
to stay on the right side of Google. You review the report; I (or you) ship the
proposals. This is the responsible "never-stop" — monitoring + proposals, not
unsupervised mass publishing.
